Overview

What Is eHallPass?

eHallPass (now known as Securly Pass) is a digital hall pass management system used by K–12 schools to replace paper hall passes. It lets students request permission to leave the classroom digitally, while teachers and staff can approve, monitor, and manage passes in real time.

Instead of writing out a paper slip, students submit a request on a school device or their phone. Teachers see the request, approve or deny it, and administrators get a full picture of student movement across the building — all logged automatically.

The system was designed to improve school safety, reduce hall congestion, and give staff better visibility without extra paperwork.

Step by Step

How eHallPass Works

The process is quick and straightforward for both students and teachers.

01
🖥️

Student Logs In

Students sign in to eHallPass using their school Google account or district credentials on any school device.

02
📝

Request a Pass

The student selects their destination (restroom, nurse, office, library, etc.) and submits a request to their teacher.

03
✅

Teacher Approves

The teacher sees the request on their dashboard and taps to approve or deny. The pass is instantly activated.

04
🚶

Student Travels

The student shows their active pass on their device if stopped in the hallway. The timer counts down automatically.

05
🔔

Pass Closes

When the student returns, they end the pass. If time runs out, the teacher is automatically notified.

06
📊

Data Is Logged

All pass activity is stored in a dashboard. Admins can review patterns, frequency, and duration for any student.

Pro Tips

Student Tips for Using eHallPass

Make the most of your digital hall pass with these helpful tips.

⏰

Check Your Pass Limits

Most schools set a maximum number of passes per day or period. Keep an eye on how many you've used so you don't run out when you really need one.

📱

Request Before You Need It

Submit your pass request a couple of minutes early during a natural break in the lesson. This avoids disruption and gets you approved faster.

🔄

Always End Your Pass

When you return, make sure to close your pass. Leaving it open will alert your teacher and may count against you on future requests.

🗺️

Pick the Right Destination

Always select the correct destination. Choosing the wrong location can cause confusion and may be flagged by administrators reviewing pass logs.

⚡

Be Quick & Respectful

The timer on your pass is real. Try to complete your trip efficiently. Repeated overtime alerts can affect how teachers view future pass requests.

💬

Talk to Your Teacher

If you have a medical reason for needing more passes than usual, speak with your teacher or nurse privately. They can adjust your settings in the system.
